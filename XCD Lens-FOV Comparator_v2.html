Method: FOV = 2·atan( crop_dimension / (2 × focal_length) ), computed against the active crop's dimensions, fitted inside the X2D's 43.8 × 32.9mm native sensor area. FF-equivalent focal length uses the 0.79× crop factor. Zoom lenses (E-series, 35-75) expose a range slider. Image Overlay treats the uploaded photo as the full frame shot at the "Shot at" focal length, then draws each selected lens's frame as 1/focal-length scaling, cropped to the active ratio.
OEM cross-check: Lenses use Hasselblad's measured effective focal lengths rather than nominal marketing numbers — verified against Hasselblad's own published FOV table, matching to within rounding on all diag/hor/vert values.
Note: figures are geometric approximations from nominal focal lengths — real optics have field curvature and slight breathing that this simulator does not model.
